Hypothesis 3: Regional Career Length Differences

Research Question: How do career lengths differ across regions, and are certain regions more effective at developing long-lasting professional players?

Hypothesis: Eastern regions (LCK, LPL) produce players with longer average careers than Western regions (LEC, LCS) due to more established infrastructure, larger player bases, and more structured developmental systems.
