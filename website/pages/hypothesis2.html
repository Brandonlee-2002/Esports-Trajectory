Hypothesis 2: Tier 2 to Tier 1 Transition Rates

Research Question: How often do players successfully transition from Tier 2 developmental leagues to Tier 1 professional play?

Hypothesis: The majority of Tier 2 players never reach Tier 1 competition. Those who do often make the transition within their first few professional seasons, suggesting a critical developmental window for player promotion.
