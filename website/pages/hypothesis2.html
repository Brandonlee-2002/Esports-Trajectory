Hypothesis 2: Tier 2 to Tier 1 Transition Rates

Research Question: How often do players successfully transition from Tier 2 developmental leagues to Tier 1 professional play?

Hypothesis: The majority of Tier 2 players never reach Tier 1 competition. Those who do typically make the transition within their first 2 years, suggesting a critical developmental window for player promotion.
